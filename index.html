Example of a Health Article Dangers of Consuming Antibiotics Without a Doctor's Prescription

Antibiotics are a type of drug that is often used to treat various diseases caused by bacterial infections. Usually, if the infection is still mild, the doctor does not need to prescribe antibiotics.

Meanwhile, for cases of severe bacterial infections, the new doctor will prescribe antibiotics. Other conditions that require antibiotics are people with weak immune conditions, for example people with HIV or cancer.
